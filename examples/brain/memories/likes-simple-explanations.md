When something gets explained to me, I get the most out of a short, concrete walkthrough
— plain words, small steps, no dense terminology up front.

**Why:** I retain a simple explanation on first read; a dense one means I re-read it
twice before it lands.
**How to apply:** Explain things in plain, everyday language and keep steps small and
concrete before adding any nuance.
