Pixel Farm is a cozy farming sim built in Godot 4, aimed at itch.io first and Steam once
it's stable. Solo project — art, code, and builds are all on me.

Builds go out through the [[godot-export-pipeline]] for Windows, Mac, and Linux.

**Why:** This is the active project; most of my day-to-day questions trace back to it.
**How to apply:** Assume questions about "the game" mean Pixel Farm unless I say
otherwise.
